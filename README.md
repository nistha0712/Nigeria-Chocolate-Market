# Nigeria Chocolate Market Opportunity Analysis 🍫📊

An end-to-end data science and market entry analysis exploring the feasibility, attractiveness, and go-to-market strategy for launching a chocolate product line in Nigeria.

---

## 📌 Executive Summary

* **Business Question:** Should we enter the Nigerian chocolate market, and if so, how?
* **Recommendation:** **Go with Caution.** Focus on urban hubs (Lagos, Abuja) with mass-market pricing and local manufacturing partnerships to hedge against supply chain volatility and import duties.
* **Key Findings:**
  1. **Macro Trends:** Urbanization and a growing middle class drive long-term demand despite high inflation.
  2. **Supply Dynamics:** Strong domestic cocoa production provides potential sourcing advantages.
  3. **Consumer Drivers:** Price sensitivity dominates purchasing decisions; value packaging and affordable price points are essential.

---

## 📂 Datasets Used

* **World Development Indicators (WDI):** Macroeconomic indicators including GDP per capita, population growth, urbanization rates, and inflation.
* **FAOSTAT Data:** Agricultural production statistics, specifically domestic cocoa production trends in West Africa.

---

## 🛠️ Analysis & Methodology

1. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Cleaned macroeconomic and agricultural time-series data to identify long-term economic trajectories.
2. **Market Attractiveness Scoring:** Evaluated market potential using population growth, economic stability, and raw material availability.
3. **Consumer & Pricing Analysis:** Assessed purchasing power and affordability thresholds to define optimal product tiers.
4. **Strategy Formulation:** Developed a phased Go-To-Market (GTM) roadmap, SWOT matrix, and risk mitigation framework.

## 🚀 Go-to-Market Strategy

* **Target Audience:** Urban young professionals and middle-income households.
* **Pricing Tier:** Mass-market low-unit-price packaging alongside a premium gift tier.
* **Distribution Channels:** Modern retail chains in major metropolitan areas paired with traditional distributor networks.
